Revolutionizing online shopping with a sleek,
intuitive front-end interface.


"ShopEz" is an online shopping platform aiming to enhance user experience by revamping its front-end interface.
This project focuses on improving navigation, aesthetics, and usability to create a seamless and visually appealing shopping experience for customers.
Key features include responsive design for intuitive navigation, optimized product displays, and enhanced checkout process.
ShopEz's main objective is prioritizing user satisfaction and engagement.
